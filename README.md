Rather than attempting to determine whether someone is truthful, deceptive, or guilty, ARSHIF helps users think critically by organizing evidence, highlighting relationships, tracking inconsistencies, and encouraging verification through transparent, explainable analysis. ARSHIF is built around the principles of intellectual honesty, privacy, and evidence-based reasoning. By combining local AI-assisted analysis, an evidence vault, claim tracking, editorial pattern recognition, timeline visualization, and provenance research tools, it serves as a digital research companion for students, educators, journalists, investigators, attorneys, researchers, and everyday citizens seeking to understand complex information better. Evidence can evolve from Observed to Repeated, Corroborated, Verified, Contradicted, or Unresolved, reinforcing the idea that careful investigation is a process rather than an instant answer.
