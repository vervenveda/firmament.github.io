Before You Sign Anything

Some programs can place a lien, mortgage, or repayment obligation on a home. Pause and ask these questions:

  • Does this create a debt, lien, or mortgage against the property?
  • What happens if I move, need long-term care, or pass away?
  • Can my family keep the home? Is there a repayment clause?
  • Are there fees, penalties, or “contractor” requirements?
  • Can I get this in writing and review it with a trusted person?

If you feel pressured, stop. Get a second opinion from a trusted counselor or legal aid office.
