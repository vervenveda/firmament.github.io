Reference desk notice

Legal Information, Not Legal Representation

Firmament is a public educational and organizational resource. It does not provide legal advice, create an attorney-client relationship, determine the truth of a claim, or replace qualified professional counsel. Laws, procedures, deadlines, benefits, and eligibility rules can vary by place and change over time.

  • Verify consequential information through current official sources.
  • Consult a licensed attorney or qualified professional about a specific matter.
  • Protect sensitive personal information before saving, printing, or sharing records.

The librarian’s principle

A library should help people think—not decide for them.

Organize the record carefully. Preserve uncertainty honestly. Let evidence remain distinct from assumption.
