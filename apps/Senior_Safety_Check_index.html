Before You Sign Anything

Some programs and contracts can risk your money or your home. This checklist helps you slow down and stay safe.

  • What does it cost today — and what could it cost later?
  • Does it put a lien, loan, or mortgage on your home?
  • Can you cancel? Is it in writing?
  • Are you being pressured to “act now” or pay with gift cards?
  • Can a trusted family member or advisor review it with you?
